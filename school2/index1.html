For schools

Easy parent-teacher-student communication

Parent-teacher collaboration and engagement as simple as WhatsApp
Get Started
Core features for school

The app in school name will be available in both mobile app & web login

Make school-related announcements, news updates to parents easily as messages

Teachers can communicate to parents class section-wise for any circulars or homework

Individual parents can be contacted by message privately by school administration or teacher

Teachers & School administration can share documents, images or videos easily

School administration can track school transport like bus or van with geo-tracking facility

Communication Channel

Easily communicate with parents, staff & teachers within a dedicated platform for the school, which enables you to communicate in different forms such as message, geo-tracking, image, video or document.

Data Security

At most priority given to ensuring the privacy of the data with the signing of a Non-Disclosure Agreement followed by technological security checkpoints such as; encrypted messages, dedicated cloud storage, and the contents shared are in the non-downloadable format enhancing data security further.

Customer Support

We ensure personalized onboarding & training for teachers and parents from the implementation stage at the premises as and when required, along with dedicated account managers who shall be available on call for any assistance.
