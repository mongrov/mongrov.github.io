Grievances

Citizens can post their grievances to the respective departments to get a swift response and solution. The request can also be sent with additional context including identity & location.

Public assistance

Citizens can request assistance related to various government initiatives. Simplifies government paper works and streamlines existing processes.

Public Records

Simplify Management and access to public records, by publishing them to BlockChain. Ensures authenticity, access and control of public records to eliminate fake certificates and provides simple and clear access to citizens.

Application Services

A single platform for citizens to apply for various government applications digitally thus access to government services and updated status of enquiries.
